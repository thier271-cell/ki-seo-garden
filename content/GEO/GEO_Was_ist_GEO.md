## SEO vs. GEO – der fundamentale Shift

Laut Backlinko GEO Guide (April 2026):

| Von | Zu |
|-----|-----|
| SEO = Google Search | SEO = multi-surface visibility (Search, AI/LLMs, social) |
| Success = ranking for keywords | Success = being found across Search + Chat |
| Backlinks to pass PageRank | Backlinks + brand mentions + co-citations |
| Traffic as core KPI | Visibility, influence, conversions across touchpoints |

**Kernaussage Backlinko:** "I'm not saying GEO replaces SEO. But it does help reframe how you think about discovery."

## 7-Step GEO Action Plan (Backlinko)

1. **Nail SEO Basics** – Crawlability, Indexability, CWV, E-E-A-T
2. **Build Mentions & Co-Citations** – Brand Mentions ohne Link zahlen
3. **Go Multi-Platform** – Reddit (#1 cited), YouTube, LinkedIn
4. **Find What AI Cites** – Manuell testen: ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity
5. **Answer Audience Questions** – Conversational Prompts, nicht nur Keywords
6. **Structure for Generative Engines** – One idea per paragraph, clear headings
7. **Track LLM Visibility** – Semrush AI Visibility Toolkit, ZipTie
